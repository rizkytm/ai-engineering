## Papers

### Foundational
- [Attention Is All You Need (Vaswani et al., 2017)](https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.03762) — The original Transformer paper
- [BERT: Pre-training of Deep Bidirectional Transformers (Devlin et al., 2018)](https://arxiv.org/abs/1810.04805) — Bidirectional pre-training
- [GPT-2: Language Models are Unsupervised Multitask Learners (Radford et al., 2019)](https://cdn.openai.com/better-language-models/language_models_are_unsupervised_multitask_learners.pdf) — Generative pre-training at scale
- [Language Models are Few-Shot Learners (Brown et al., 2020)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2005.14165) — GPT-3 and in-context learning

### Embeddings
- [Word2Vec (Mikolov et al., 2013)](https://arxiv.org/abs/1301.3781) — Embeddings from neural networks
- [Sentence-BERT (Reimers & Gurevych, 2019)](https://arxiv.org/abs/1908.10084) — Sentence embeddings for similarity
