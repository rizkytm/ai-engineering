## Quick Reference

### Rate Tiers (US Market, 2025)
| Level | Hourly Rate | Typical Projects |
|-------|-------------|------------------|
| Junior | $50–100 | Simple integrations, data processing |
| Mid-level | $100–175 | RAG systems, custom AI features |
| Senior | $175–300 | Complex architectures, consulting |
| Specialist | $200–400 | Fine-tuning, MLOps, enterprise |

### Proposal Response Time
- First response: Within 24 hours
- Full proposal: Within 3–5 business days
- Follow-up: 3–5 days after sending proposal
- Weekly updates: Every Friday (no exceptions)

### Minimum Requirements for a Proposal
- [ ] Signed NDA (if sharing proprietary info)
- [ ] Discovery call completed
- [ ] Scope defined (in + out)
- [ ] Timeline realistic
- [ ] Payment terms stated
- [ ] Change process documented
